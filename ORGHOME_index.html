About Me

Muhibu is a geospatial analyst and developer, currently working with INRAE (Aix-en-Provence), a renowned public research institute in France. He holds a BSc in Geoscience (2015) and an MSc in Georesources (2018) from the University of Tlemcen, Algeria. Motivated by his strong passion for environmental subjects, he made a conscientious decision to pursue a Master of Science degree in Environmental Hazards and Risks Management at the University of CÃ´te d'Azur (Nice, France) from 2019 to 2021.

Muhibu's expertise lies in geospatial analysis using remote sensing and GIS softwares (GEE, QGis and AcrGis) and application development using Django framework, Goserver, leaflet with a strong command of Python, R, SQL as well as Html, CSS, javascript . His skills allow him to effectively analyze geospatial data and create robust applications for various purposes.

As a geospatial analyst and developer at INRAE, he makes valuable contribution to the company and further enhancing his professional expertise.
